kybernḗtēs
kybernē̂tai

Cybernetist. Literally, “cyberneticist”. Still more literally, “gubernator”, “governor”, “steersman”. Specifically, helmsman or pilot. No relation to ‹Khyber› or online sex, I think. Cognate of ‹Kubernetes›, ‹cyberneticist›, ‹cybernetist›, ‹cybernetician›, ‹gubernator›, ‹governor›; related to the cognates of ‹cyber›, ‹cyber‑›.
